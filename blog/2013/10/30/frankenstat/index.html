FrankenStat

30-10-2013

I’ve been trying to make my own thermostat for years. A few month back I stumbled across the Arduino platform and after a bit of playing around with it, this looked to be the platform on which I could make this work. Because the first prototype was a bunch of wires, I named it the FrankenStat.

What I want from my thermostat is:

  • Having a REST API to control the thermostat and get statistics (I love graphs!)
  • Being able to control the thermostat manually with buttons.
  • It should be programmable (have multiple temperature settings per day.)
  • I should be able to adjust the temperature temperarily until the next programmed temperature setting.
  • Be able to place it anywhere in my living room and control the CV wireless.
